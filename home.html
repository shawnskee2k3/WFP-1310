Card of the week

Mr. Invincible

Featured Character – Kamui Katsuragi

A youngster with a big mouth and the skill to back it up. He’s very enthusiastic and energetic about Vanguard and doesn’t care who his opponent is so long as they are not weak in his eyes. He hates being looked down upon because of his size and age. He also has a crush on Aichi ‘s little sister, Emi , and constantly vies for her attention, even going as far as to refer to Aichi as his older brother.
