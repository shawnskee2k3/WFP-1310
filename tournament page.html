The Cardfight!! Vanguard World Championship is back again this year! The competition gets tougher with the new expansions! Do you have what it takes to be the Champion?

Cardfight!! Vanguard World Championship 2013 features Open Regional Qualifiers across the world. These Open Regional Qualifiers will be held between August to October. Winners from each Open Regional Qualifier will proceed to compete for the chance to represent their respective regions at the Continental Championships (North America, Europe or Asia-Oceania) between October and November.

The winners of the Continental Championship will represent their regions at the World Championship 2013 Finals in Japan on January 12, 2014.

All participants in the Open Regional Qualifiers will receive an exclusive promotional card and card storage box.

* Please note that all tournament dates, locations an details are subjected to changes.
